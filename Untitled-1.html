2021/12/13 歡迎各位新生加入我們！可以到工程管E315詢問～或按聯絡我們～

歡迎各位來到勤益電機系E315實驗室!本實驗室的指導老師為-宋文財教授，本研究室的研究領域包括： 物聯網相關應用、無線感測網路、生理訊號感測與處理、資料融合、系統理論與控制、類神經網路應用、系統晶片。

目前本研究室的成員有18人，包括：6位日間部碩士生以及4位在職碩士生、還有8位專題生。在過去幾年內，本研究室在學術論文發表、專利、技轉、產學合作計畫、政府贊助的研究計畫以及技術競賽上皆有優秀的表現。

Welcome to the E315 laboratory of Chin-Yi Electrical Machinery Department! The instructor of this laboratory is Professor Wen-tsai Sung. The research fields of this laboratory include: Internet of Things related applications, wireless sensing networks, physiological signal sensing and processing, data fusion, system theory and control, and neural network-like applications , System Plastics. At present, there are 18 members in this research, including: 6 daytime master students, 4 on-the-job master students, and 8 special subjects. In the past few years, our laboratory has performed well in academic paper publications, patents, technology transfer, industry-university cooperation projects, government-funded research projects, and technology competitions.
